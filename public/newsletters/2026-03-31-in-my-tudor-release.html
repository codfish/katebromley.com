Love can make you lose your head...

In My Tudor Era is out today

I cannot believe I get to say that!

This book has lived in my head (and heart) for a long time and now it's officially out in the world. If you love an irreverent time-travel romance with STEM, steam, and group chat banter... I wrote this for you!

 

Sharp banter. Forbidden love. Questionable decisions (the fun kind).

Lily’s life is turned upside down when she suddenly finds herself trapped in the body of Catherine Howard, King Henry VIII's doomed fifth wife. Can she make it out of Tudor England with her head and her heart intact? Slip into this raunchy, irreverent time travel romance!
